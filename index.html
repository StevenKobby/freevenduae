Still Have Questions?

Yes, 100% free. Blue Box supplies, delivers, installs, stocks and maintains your vending machine at absolutely zero cost to your organisation. We generate revenue through product sales — you enjoy all the benefits with no investment required.

Blue Box retains ownership of all machines. You simply provide the space — we handle everything else. If you ever need the machine removed, we take care of that too with no fees or penalties.

After completing the initial site survey and signing the partnership agreement, most installations are completed within 3–5 working days. Our engineers work efficiently to minimise any disruption to your workplace.

We operate across all seven UAE emirates — Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah, and Umm Al Quwain. Our logistics and service network ensures timely restocking and support wherever you are.

Our team carries out regular scheduled restocking visits based on usage. We also monitor stock levels remotely and can dispatch additional refills as needed. For technical issues, our engineers respond within 4 hours.

Our machines accept cash (AED), all major debit and credit cards, and contactless/NFC payments including Apple Pay, Samsung Pay and Google Pay. Cashless-only configurations are also available on request.
